Centilion
Centilion has 101 noses and 101 feet long. It has a wingspan of 303 feet, it has 2 eyes on 1 side. They have twice as more dangeres venom than a scauldron. They are snake like but has a wing for swimming and for flying. They can live up to 4000 years. Centilion have eggs each year and have 101 eggs in a year. Centilion's are fast growing dragons because they are only 1-7 in when they are born.
